Terms & Conditions

By using Glospot ("the platform"), you agree to these Terms & Conditions. Please read them carefully before making any reservation or listing a property.

Glospot acts as an intermediary between guests and property owners. We are not a hotel chain or property operator — we facilitate discovery, booking and communication between guests and hosts.

Booking Policy

When you make a booking through Glospot, you enter into a direct agreement with the property owner. Glospot confirms your booking on behalf of the property.

  • All bookings are subject to availability at the time of confirmation.
  • A booking confirmation email is sent immediately upon successful reservation.
  • Guests must present valid ID at check-in.
  • The number of guests must not exceed the stated capacity of the property.

Cancellation Policy

Each property sets its own cancellation policy, which is clearly displayed before you confirm your booking. Glospot supports the following standard policy types:

  • Free cancellation: Full refund if cancelled at least 48 hours before check-in.
  • Partial refund: 50% refund if cancelled at least 24 hours before check-in.
  • Non-refundable: No refund upon cancellation — displayed clearly at booking.

To cancel, go to My Account → My Bookings and select the relevant reservation.

Payments

Glospot supports two payment models, set independently by each property:

  • Pay online now: Payment is collected securely at booking. Funds are released to the property after check-in.
  • Pay at hotel: No payment is collected online. The guest pays the property directly on arrival. A valid card may be required to guarantee the reservation.

Supported currencies include EUR, USD, GBP and Albanian Lek (ALL). Currency conversion is indicative — the property's base currency applies for final payment.

Reviews

Glospot is committed to honest, trustworthy reviews. Only guests who have completed a verified stay booked through our platform may leave a review.

  • Reviews are submitted via the guest's personal account after check-out.
  • Reviews are moderated before being published to ensure they comply with our guidelines.
  • Properties cannot remove or edit guest reviews.
  • Review ratings (1–5 stars) and comments are visible on the property listing page.

Privacy Policy

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services: name, email, phone number, country of origin and booking history. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Property Verification

Every property listed on Glospot goes through a verification process before appearing in search results. This includes:

  • Verification of ownership or management rights for the property
  • Review of photos, descriptions and amenity claims for accuracy
  • Confirmation of the property's address and contact details
  • Ongoing monitoring of guest reviews for quality assurance

Verified properties are marked with a verification badge. If you believe a property is inaccurately described, please report it through the listing page.

  • Glospot reserves the right to suspend or remove listings that violate these terms or receive consistently poor reviews.
